About Us

This started as class project. Initially we thought about doing a simulation, but our friend Steff was big Into writing and we decided to dedicate the site to her and ourselves to write on our stories.

We believe in the power of community and collaboration, and we encourage you to engage with us and share your thoughts and ideas. Our goal is to create a space where anyone can connect, And learn from each other.
